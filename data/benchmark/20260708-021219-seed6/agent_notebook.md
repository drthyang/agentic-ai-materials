Replacing Zn with Mg/Ca (smaller ionic radius, higher electronegativity for Mg; larger radius, lower electronegativity for Ca) and Sn with Ge (smaller ionic radius, higher electronegativity) will enable precise band-gap engineering. Target family: Cu2(II)(IV)S4 where II={Zn,Mg,Ca}, IV={Sn,Ge} (avoid overoxidized combinations). Cite: 2016 Ge-doping study (Nanoscale), 2025 kesterite preparation (Japanese JAP).
